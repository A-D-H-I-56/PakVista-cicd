Welcome to Hunza Valley

Famous Landmarks

Hunza Valley is a mountainous valley in the Gilgit-Baltistan region. It is known for its breathtaking scenery, the turquoise waters of Attabad Lake, the historic Baltit Fort, and the majestic Rakaposhi viewpoint.
