Welcome to Lahore – City of Gardens

Famous Landmarks

Lahore is the cultural heart of Pakistan. It is home to magnificent Mughal architecture including the Badshahi Mosque, Lahore Fort, and the beautiful Shalimar Gardens. The city is famous for its rich history and vibrant food culture.
